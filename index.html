Start-up Experience

I chose the later option and haven't regreted it so far. Manage My Power (MMP) was created to solve the problem that too many solar owners had: lack of returns. Our solution is built to guide businesses in their energy investments, and save them energy with what they already have. My role was initially to overview contracts, identify leads, negotiate contracts and design custom solutions for larger projects. It wasn't easy bringing money in to continue R&D and pay ourselves. So, I started another venture doing some IT consulting for SMEs looking to automate their business processes. Even if it was successful on a financial level, my associate and I had to part ways. Now MMP is mostly a B2B platform we rely on energy efficiency specialists to on sale our solution. My remote role is currently to identify leads, build business profiles, overview operations and IT work.
